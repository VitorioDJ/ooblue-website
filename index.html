Vitorio De Janeiro

Vitorio is a modern day Alchemist and Visionary, with the unique gift of inspiring & guiding people towards the state of full Inner Freedom. In his work, he supports the upgrade of consciousness on individual and collective level, so that most lofty goals are reached from the place of joy and minimum stress.

In his “previous life” Vitorio ran several international businesses between the United States, Brazil and Europe, including Digital Animation and Real Estate Development.

After years of business and material success, Vitorio moved to Bali and committed to in-depth study of subjects that fascinated him since childhood - Astrology, Shamanism, Alchemy and Sound. He studied with various Masters around the world and eventually co-created Alchemy School in Bali with High Alchemists of the Federation of Damanhur. During this period, Vitorio developed advanced and precise tools for working with subtle energetic fields and subconscious mind.

Vitorio combines his experience as an entrepreneur with ancient wisdom of Astrology, Shamanism and Alchemy. His Soul & Business Blueprint System helps business leaders worldwide, shifting priorities and redirecting their business to align with their Inner Purpose while maintaining top effectiveness and productivity rooted in deep inner joy.

Evia Dalbina

Evia is a Designer, modern day Alchemist and Visionary with a special gift of seeing straight into a person's soul as well as future tendencies in the world.

Having followed the path of art and beauty and passion for transforming spaces Evia first became an innovative interior designer, creating concepts for hospitality business, blending nature, luxury and eco-responsible principles. Operating at all levels of a project from concept to management , Evia accumulated skills, knowledge and recognition in the material world till the moment when an unexpected deep soul calling brought her to make a life changing choice to leave the successful and established career in South of France and move to Bali where her children could receive a more magical and heart centered schooling and life experience.

From this new perspective of life Evia continued to explore new landscapes of inner worlds and discover the connection with the world of Crystals, Quantum Healing, Alchemy and elevated States of Consciousness.

She travelled all over the world sourcing knowledge in its most authentic form in order to be able to help people redesign their lives and living spaces in harmony with their true essence while developing an unique approach of transforming inner states with principles of quantum alchemy and deep re-programming on a cellular level. Her method and enthusiasm has helped countless people see their true potential and start making choices towards fully embracing life of joy and magic!

Having a clear understanding of current need for more fulfillment and inner peace alongide with material expression and success, Evia is specializing in new paradigm design and leadership development working with leaders who are dedicated to create dynamic change in the world.
