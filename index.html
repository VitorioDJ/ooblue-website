Vitorio De Janeiro

Vitorio is a contemporary alchemist and visionary dedicated to exploring inner freedom and applied consciousness. Evia Aur

Evia is a designer and visionary. Her work explores the intersection of art, space, and inner experience. After many years dedicated to creating design concepts inspired by nature, her journey has shifted toward a more subtle exploration of perception, consciousness, and transformation. Through STELL-ART and VIBE-SPACES projects, she creates artistic experiences that invite presence, remembrance, and connection between the visible and the invisible.
