Course Details

Money Detox is an 8-week Transformational Journey with Vitorio & Evia.

Every week will begin with an interactive live webinar that combines teaching and a personal energetic upgrade. At the end of each webinar, you’ll receive material to work with during the week - instructional videos, practical tools and guided meditations. You’ll have a concrete, hands-on task to accomplish during each week, that will help you integrate and embody the week’s work and prepare you for the following week’s journey. Following through week by week, your upgrades will stack up and build upon each other.

All webinars will be recorded and available to watch online for unlimited time, so you can attend the course when it's convenient for you.
