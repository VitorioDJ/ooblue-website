Are you ready to be free of your deepest fears?

There are few topics in life that we know are inevitable, but we keep masterfully avoiding them, thus feeling overwhelmed when the actual life situation occurs.

The top 3 favorites are:
Change, Loss and Death

We all have experienced a number of shocking changes in our lives. The way we face them largely determines the quality of our life. Either we’re ready to embrace them as a surfer embraces the wave and we keep adapting our path, or else we’re knocked down by the unexpected wave of events, overwhelmed by the whirlpool, desperately lost, disoriented and hurt.

Life is offering us both challenges and opportunities. Once we know how to embrace sudden change, brutal loss and the one and only certainty in this existence - our own death, we are equipped to live joyful and fulfilled lives, transitioning with ease and grace whatever events may happen on our path.

Living in full readiness for anything is the mastery of Life described in all ancient traditions and taught throughout ages to selected ones.

Today you have an opportunity to access this knowledge right from your own home, brought in a simple, efficient and loving way by someone who has gone through his own death & rebirth, and survived numerous extreme life situations, including the loss of his child. Joseph Pepe Danza will share with you powerful tools of understanding LIFE, DEATH and CHANGE in a way that was never before possible.

He will hold your hand through 6 weeks of diving deep into 5 stages of Transformation and bring you to Transcendance on the other side - empowered, relaxed and ready to face life in all its beauty!

This course is for you if you want to:
  • Empower yourself to be “ready for life”
  • Understand the mechanics of facing profound life transitions
  • Get the tools for surviving challenging times, that you can use to help yourself or others
  • Gain inner peace, confidence and trust in the process of being human
  • Use the power of the group to transform your fears
How Does It Work?

In the course of 6 weeks, Pepe will teach about Death as inseparable part of Life. Throughout the course you will systematically build confidence and understanding about all life transitions, from change to loss, and learn how to transcend them with awareness. Pepe will lovingly guide you to embrace and understand the concept of death, to better live every moment of this life.

Week by week, we will go through 5 stages of grief, that follow an unexpected event in life, assess our reactions and fears, and then align our thoughts and emotions through toning, guided meditations and breathing exercises inspired by Qi-gong, Zen and Tibetan Buddhist practices. Weekly sound-healing tracks will stay with you to be used as tools whenever need for alignment arises.

Course Details

Falling In Love With Death is a 6-week Transformational Journey with Joseph Pepe Danza.

Every week opens with a 90-min webinar that combines teaching, sound and physical movement. At the end of each webinar, you’ll have a homework - a concrete, hands-on task to accomplish during each week, that will help you integrate and embody the week’s work and prepare you for next week’s journey. Following through week by week, your upgrades will stack up and build upon each other.

All webinars are recorded and available to watch for unlimited time, so you can attend the course when it's convenient for you.

Joseph Pepe Danza

Course Facilitator

Joseph Pepe Danza
Sound Healer, Zen Practitioner
Pepe Danza, about the course:

"At the end of my years in India I caught some mysterious disease that supposedly (according to some village doctor) would probably kill me... slowly. My body stopped digesting food and I was constantly running a fever. I lost so much weight that I went down to just over fifty kilos. Throughout the process I felt a growing sense of inner peace and acceptance that stayed forever in my spirit. Maybe that's why Death decided to let me go for now. Many years later on my long journey with plant teachers, I was guided into a full death experience, which started rather terrifyingly and ended in the deepest bliss and expanded consciousness I've ever experienced...

I have also lost many dear ones, and went through many little deaths in relationships and instances of loss and change.

Throughout all of this, my deepening practice of Buddhism, Taoism, Yoga, Shamanism and Tantra gave me the tools not only of profoundly internalizing the truth of impermanence, but also of appreciating its beauty and poetry, and not only befriending Death but even lovingly feeling its presence as a friendly reminder of living intensely and gratefully every moment.

Throughout these six weeks we will talk about Death and Fear, exploring tools to sail through these stormy times that we are collectively experiencing, in full presence, inner power and reliance, and why not, even gratitude."

Pepe was born in Uruguay and left his homeland at the young age of twenty one. After years of wandering the planet, studying, performing and practicing different traditional music and instruments (Sitar and Bansuri in India, Brazilian Percussion, African Djembe, Guitar in South America and Spain, etc.), as well as Jazz and Rock/Pop music, he finally landed in Japan, where he lived for three years dedicated intensively to the study of the Zen Flute (Shakuhachi). He later moved to Canada where he lived for twenty five years teaching and performing and further on to Israel for six years where he opened a Yoga School with his partner.

Parallel to his musical studies he also pursued a path of Spirit that includes years in India practicing Yoga and Tibetan Buddhism, Zen meditation in Japan, the Gurdjieff System in North America, Dervish practices with Turkish Sufis, Sound Healing in Bali and Shamanic knowledge with South and North American First Nations Medicine people.

Pepe has extensive international experience teaching Meditation, Yoga, Sound Healing, Tantra and Daoist practices besides being a sought after performer and recording artist. He was a founder and Artistic Director of the Sacred Music Festival in Vancouver, Canada, for four years, and has won several awards for his compositions for film and theater.

Pepe lives from the deep conviction and experience of the fact that Music is a powerful transformative force, and that Humanity and our dear Planet Earth are ready for a shift to a higher frequency. He is here to serve that Work, in whatever way, whichever place he is called.
