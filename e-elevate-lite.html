How Does It Work?

We begin by Vitorio & Evia scanning your inner and outer state and provide your personal Prosperity Blueprint - an in-depth report of the current state of your mind, emotions and energy, and the suggested way forward. This report will show you exactly which aspects of your inner world need to be addressed, and it serves as a map of your journey.

Once you have the map - the work begins.

At the beginning of each week, you’ll receive material to work with during the week - instructional videos, practical tools and guided meditations. You’ll have a concrete, hands-on task to accomplish during each week, that will help you integrate and embody the week’s work and prepare you for the following week’s journey. Following through week by week, your upgrades will stack up and build upon each other.

We communicate through recorded voice messages. At the beginning of each week, Vitorio & Evia will record a set of tasks for you to do during the week. As you go through your weekly tasks, the underlying issues in your life will immediately begin to shift.

You are encouraged to get a blank notebook and dedicate it to be your Elevate Journal. You’ll track your progress, writing down all daily events and syncronicities that stand out. Also, you’ll keep track of any questions coming up. Make sure to write a short reflection at the end of each day, to recap the effects, messages and the meaning of the current day.

At the end of each week, you'll record a voice message with any questions you may have from your current week’s work. Vitorio & Evia will review your message, answer all your questions and use these insights in creating your next week’s tasks.

At the end of each month we will review your work, understand where you stand with your topics and reflect on the progress you’ve made. At this point we decide whether your work is finished for now, or we continue the process for another month. If we’re continuing the program, you will get new material and set of tasks for the coming period.

This profound inner work will ripple through your entire reality. Almost immediately you will notice that the world seems to operate very differently than what you knew before. There is less effort. Less attachment. More ease. And more flow.

And there's an empowering feeling that you are becoming a real Creator Of Your Life.
