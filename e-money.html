Unlock Your Personal Prosperity

Do you have a sense of unease when it comes to money? Are you dreading paying bills and even just logging-on into your bank account? So many doubts and pressure since early age keep misguiding you, so to never really get full control over your finances. The hooks go deep and their function is to keep you in a perpetual state of servitude.

The good news is that there’s a way out.

In our Money Detox sessions we focus on those aspects and false believes that constantly block the money flow in your life. We find them, help you to unhook them and we guide you in completely changing your reality around the topic of money.

By following our detox process, you will recover the lost key to your own wealth, and get back to the original state of empowered and prosperous being you were always meant to be.

How Does It Work?

Your Unique Gift session is 90-min long in total, and is usually done in 2 parts:

First part is an on-line call where both Evia and Vitorio connect with you on a call and assess your current situation. They scan your field, looking for any blockages & issues, and they map their origin and exact location.

Second part of the session is an off-line, energetic treatment where Evia and Vitorio remove the blockages energetically while you meditate.

On the following day, Evia and Vitorio connect with you on a quick follow up call.

Altogether it's a 90-min engagement.
