Light UP

Ignite the light that you are

In the world of infinite possibilities, what would you choose to be? Would you add more qualities to your Self or you’d rather take some old ones off? Or would you maybe just - BE?

What we found might be needed the most in these times of transition is - keeping the feeling of lightness. Both feeling lighter and embodying light.

There are many ways to get there and we will share with you our newest discoveries in this topic and create an empowering space so that together we can Light-UP ourselves and the world around us.

In this new series, we will co-create the field and hold space for each other with less words and more presence for what IS. We’ll work on accepting Life as it comes, and we’ll observe when our “perfect ideas (our conditioning) tries to interfere and teach Life how it "should" be.

Join us each Friday and see if more light and lightness is what you need in your life right now!
