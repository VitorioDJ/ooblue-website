How Does It Work?

Elevate! package consists of 4 private 90-min sessions with Vitorio & Evia:

Session 1: Where My Life Always Goes Wrong?
Session 2: What I’d Never Admit About Myself?
Session 3: What I Feel Like Doing But Shouldn’t? / What I Should Be Doing But Don’t Feel Like?
Session 4: The Change I Can’t Avoid

Before each session Evia and Vitorio scan your energy field and assess your current situation. At the session, first they guide you through the process of seeing your maze - your own private prison, made of loops, programs and obsessive behavior patterns. Next they take you through precisely formulated protocols where, through the power of your Free Will, you gradually deconstruct your maze from within, tearing down one wall at a time. As a result It harmonizes your inner state, creates feeling of lightness and spaciousness, brings clarity and confidence in life and yourself.

This profound inner work will ripple through your entire reality and almost immediately you will notice that the world seems to operate very differently than what you knew before. There is less effort. Less attachment. More ease. And more flow.

And there's an empowering feeling that you are about to become a real Creator Of Your Life.
