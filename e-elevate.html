How Does It Work?

We begin with a 30-min call where we learn which areas of your life need immediate attention. From there, we prepare a personalized inner transformation journey for you.

Vitorio & Evia will scan your life situation and provide a in-depth report of the current state of your mind, emotions and energy. This report will serve as your Personal Prosperity Blueprint. If you look at yourself as a system of pipes conducting vital energy, your blueprint will show which pipes are flowing well and which ones are "clogged" and need to be cleared. Once your personalized plan is made and your Prosperity Blueprint established, we begin the weekly process.

Every week begins with a 90-min session with Vitorio & Evia. At the session, first they guide you through the process of seeing your maze - your own private prison, made of loops, programs and obsessive behavior patterns. Next they take you through precisely formulated protocols where, through the power of your Free Will, you gradually deconstruct your maze from within, tearing down one wall at a time. As a result, your inner state is harmonized, giving you a feeling of lightness and spaciousness, offering new level of clarity & confidence in life and in yourself.

At the end of each session, you’ll receive material to work with during the week - instructional videos, practical tools and guided meditations. You’ll have a concrete, hands-on task to accomplish during each week, that will help you integrate and embody the week’s work and prepare you for the following week’s journey. Following through week by week, your upgrades will stack-up and build upon each other.
